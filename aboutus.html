Our story

With an experience of more than 15 year in salons and the developed expertise working with World's top beauty brands, the celebrity hair stylist and an entrepreneur Rashid Ali started his brand Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy at Bander Bagicha area in Patna in Bihar. Today Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy comprise of the top-class professional designers and marquee artist in the hair, beauty and makeup industry. Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy has become a brand to reckon with when it comes to out of the box gromming destination both for the male and the female. Its avant-garde, urban, current and innovative image and its excellent services have made Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy the key signature brand that drives the business of aesthetics and beauty. Since its inception, it has chosen to be the benchmark of the sector; broke the traditional concept of hairdressing and revolutionized the panorama with its powerful brand image, unique decoration, the most uninterrupted schedule and the most complete list of services, products and quality treatments at a very competitive price. This new concept also integrated work processes, technology, business management, training and marketing with the sole objective of satisfying the customer and creating a profitable, secure and lasting relationship for the community.

History of the Company

Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy salon was established 10 years ago with a single outlet at the center the happenning city of Patna in Bihar. We entered into the hair dressing and beauty industry first hand with just one single motive, Customer Care and Reinventing Fashion in hairdressing, makeup and skincare. And since then, our core value transformed into a luxe fashion brand named Blonde and Beyond Salon Academy. Our consistent quest and passion for inventing hairdressing has now become an iconic name and brand in the Hair and Beauty Industry of Patna serving millions of customers.

Design and Concept

The salon interiors are inspired from abstract and defined lines; giving the salon a very modern, plush and stylish look. The inspiration for the salon interiors comes mostly from the needs expressed by the growing awareness and more informed consumers.

While setting up the salon, we faced typical challenges that come from a very fast growing and radically changing industry. With the consumers being more informed and aware; we had to ensure that we live to the perception of ultimate in luxury yet affordable as per mindset of customers.

 

Corporate Vision

Our vision is to be a leading customer centric salon, warmly welcoming all customers for an experience which takes them on a high in terms of quality Hair, beauty & Skin services in a hygienic, relaxed, comfortable environment to make their day fabulous.
